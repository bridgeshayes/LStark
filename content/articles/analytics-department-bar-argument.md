The whiteboard in the corner of the analytics office has been erased and rewritten so many times you can still see the ghost of last month's argument underneath this month's.

That's not a metaphor. It's the actual whiteboard.

## Two Rooms, One Decision

On one side: a stats team armed with shot-quality models and a decade of tracking data. On the other: a group of former players who trust what they saw with their own eyes over what a model says should have happened.

Both sides are usually right about something. Neither side likes admitting it.

## The Argument That Works

What makes this front office different isn't that the tension has been resolved — it hasn't — it's that they've built a process that needs the argument to happen before a single roster decision gets made.
